Assumes both rasters share the same extent, resolution, and grid alignment (same sensor/date processing recommended), and are in a geographic CRS (EPSG:4326). If your data is in a projected CRS, reproject to WGS84 first. If your NDVI is stored as scaled integers (e.g. Landsat Collection 2 scales NDVI by 10000) rather than floats from -1 to 1, the threshold slider auto-scales to your data's actual range once both files load, so this works either way.
