Welcome to the Answer Plot Explorer

This map shows the Minneota trial plots for the Green Snap Story. Here's what you need to know:

  • Colors on the NDVI layers reflect the green snap, from healthy (green) to open canopy (yellow).
  • Within the plot square you can see the Relative Maturity.
  • Open canopy is caused by green snap is irregular while open canopy between range block are uniform. Try not to get them confused!
