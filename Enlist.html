This map shows the Lake Vermillion trial plots for the Soybean Enlist demo. Here's what you need to know:

  • Click on any plot square to see its treatment, application rates, and notes.
  • Use the layer toggles on the right to switch between NDVI imagery dates.
  • Colors on the NDVI layers reflect plant health, from healthy (green) to stressed (red). See Legend for more Information
  • Changed detection between both NDVI images represents herbicide effectiveness as a percentage.
