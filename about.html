What is the purpose of this website?

This website was created as my final project for my Winfield United Summer Research Technicion internship. It allows a user to virtually explore our Answer Plot trials. Each Trial showcases its unique treatments/characteristics. Using multispectral drone imagery collected throughout the summer Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) analysis can be perfromed. Crop treatments/characteristics can be compared using NDVI analysis.

How can I use this page?

Explore the select trials found within our Answer Plot. Learn about the various treatments are applied to our trials. Visually compare simular trials and see what makes them unique.

What is an Answer Plot?

Answer Plots were created by Winfield United to localize agricultural research and testing systems. Field trials test and evaulate seed varieties, crop protection products, and managment practices. Providing data driven insights to local farmers.

What is the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI)?

Multispectral images consist of images within visible wavelengths (red, green, blue) and those within the invisible wavelengths (near-infrared and red-edge).
